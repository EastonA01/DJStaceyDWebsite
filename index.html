Meet Stacey & Beverly

We're Stacey & Beverly — a husband and wife duo dedicated to making your event everything you've hoped for and more. From music to coordination, we're here to take the stress out and bring the energy in.

DJ Stacey D

DJ Stacey’s goal is simple: to create lasting memories through music. A master at reading the crowd and maintaining high energy, he ensures that every guest has a night to remember. With an extensive music library spanning all genres and decades, he customizes the playlist to match your vibe — from Rock & Roll to Country, Beach music, and more. Whether it's a wedding, private party, or corporate event, DJ Stacey delivers top-notch sound, lighting, and atmosphere.

It’s Your Day – Beverly’s Touch

Beverly not only supports the music — she’s a certified Wedding Director. From rehearsal to the couple’s send-off, Beverly coordinates every detail. She ensures everyone knows their role, assists photographers with capturing the must-have moments, and manages the timeline — from the aisle walk to the cake cutting and toasts. Her goal? A stress-free day filled with beautiful memories.
